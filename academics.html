Terms and Conditions
Placement Registration Terms

By registering for campus placements, you agree to the following terms:

  1. All information provided in the registration form must be accurate and truthful.
  2. You must maintain a minimum CGPA of 2.5 to be eligible for placements.
  3. Once registered for a placement drive, attendance is mandatory unless prior permission is obtained.
  4. You must adhere to the dress code and professional conduct during placement activities.
  5. The university reserves the right to cancel your placement registration in case of any disciplinary issues.
  6. Your personal information may be shared with potential employers for placement purposes only.
  7. You must not engage in any activity that may harm the university's relationship with recruiting companies.
Placement season for 2023 graduates begins on June 1, 2023. Make sure your profile is updated in the placement portal.
